# Game Record

# PUBG: Battlegrounds

## Summary
- **Date of Birth:** December 20, 2017
- **Engine:** Unreal Engine 4
- **Genre:** Battle Royale
- **Composer:** Tom Salta

## Release
PUBG: Battlegrounds was developed by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean company Krafton. The game was created under the direction of Brendan "PlayerUnknown" Greene, who had previously created the battle royale game mode as mods for other games. An early access version was released on Steam on March 23, 2017, and the full version launched on December 20, 2017. The game quickly became one of the most played titles on Steam, breaking concurrent player records. Mobile versions for iOS and Android were released in 2018, developed by Tencent Games.

## Monetization
PUBG transitioned to a free-to-play model in January 2022, after initially being a paid title. Revenue comes from cosmetic items including character outfits, weapon skins, emotes, and vehicle skins. Players can purchase items through the in-game store or earn them through the Survivor Pass, a seasonal battle pass system with free and premium tiers. Loot boxes called "crates" can also be obtained through gameplay or purchased with real money.
